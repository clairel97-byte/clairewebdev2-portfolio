Introduction and Registration

YouTube is a video sharing service where users can watch, like, share, comment and upload their own videos. The video service can be accessed on PCs, laptops, tablets and via mobile phones.

      Registration Steps
  1. Download YouTube App.
  2. Fill in your name and surname.
  3. Fill in your Date of birth.
  4. Fill in your desired email address.
  5. Add a password to your account.
  6. Add a phone number to the account.
  7. Review your account information.
  8. Read true the privacy and terms.
  9. Confirm your details.
  10. Now load your YouTube App.

How to Use

    Steps On How to Use
  1. You will need to type www.youtube.com into your web browser to access the website
  2. Next use the search bar at the top of the home page to search for a specific topic of tutorial
  3. After you click search, you will see a plethora of video tutorial videos based on your search parameters.
